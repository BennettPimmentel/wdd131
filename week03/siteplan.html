Healthy Breads

A bakery project focused on nutritious fruit-flavored breads.

Optional domain: healthybreads.org

Site Purpose

The website will showcase healthy, fruit-flavored breads designed for breakfast. It will provide information about ingredients, nutritional value, and ordering options.

Scenarios

  • Where can I see the different flavors of healthy breads available?
  • How can I order bread for pickup or delivery?

Color Schema

Main Colors:

  • #4CAF50 (green) – used for headings and accents.
  • #F4A261 (orange) – used for buttons and highlights.
  • #333333 (dark gray) – used for body text.

Typography

  • Montserrat – for headings.
  • Open Sans – for body text and paragraphs.
